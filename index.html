It is an escape from the real world;
a creation that narrows on one particular subject and carefully details their every move and emotion.
Photography as a hobby is perhaps the most popular of all its uses.
Cameras and films are now cheap and easy to use. ‘Instamatic’ cameras have largely eliminated the hit-or-miss techniques of yesteryears.
The use of camera phones has made life so easy and entertaining.
It has a vast usage and is one of the best modern age techniques so far.
Anybody with a pair of eyes and hands can take reasonably good pictures.
We see amateur photographers all over the place, especially in holiday resorts and recreational areas.
